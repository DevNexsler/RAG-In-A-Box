# Grandma's Kimchi Recipe

## Ingredients
- 1 napa cabbage
- 1/4 cup sea salt
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 3 tbsp gochugaru (Korean red pepper flakes)
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, grated

## Instructions
1. Cut cabbage into quarters, salt and let sit for 2 hours
2. Rinse and drain
3. Mix remaining ingredients into a paste
4. Coat cabbage thoroughly
5. Pack into jar, ferment 3-5 days at room temperature
